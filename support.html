Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is Fim It?
A1: Fim It (Framed Interactive Media It) is an app that lets you create and view interactive images using the .fim format, with tappable hotspots and built-in analytics.
Q2: What is a .fim file?
A2: A .fim file is a custom interactive image format that contains hotspots, embedded actions, and analytics tracking. It works inside the Fim It app.
Q3: What data does Fim It collect?
A3: Fim It collects only hotspot interaction analytics such as hotspot taps, link clicks, and engagement counts. It does not collect personal information like names or payment details.
Q4: Why is my analytics showing zero?
A4: Analytics only update when hotspots are added correctly, the .fim file is opened by a viewer, and the device is online to sync. If you still see zero, check your hotspot setup or contact support.
Q5: How does the free trial work?
A5: Fim It offers a 7-day free trial with full access to analytics. After the trial, continued use of analytics features requires a paid subscription.
Q6: How do I cancel my subscription?
A6: Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. You must cancel from your device’s subscription settings. The Fim It team cannot cancel subscriptions manually.
Q7: How do I report a bug or issue?
A7: You can email fimdevelopers@gmail.com with your device model, operating system version, and a description of the problem. Screenshots or screen recordings are helpful but optional.
Q8: Can I share .fim files with others?
A8: Yes. You can share .fim files via messaging apps, email, or cloud storage. The recipient needs the Fim It app installed to open them.
Q9: Is my data safe?
A9: Analytics data is stored on secure servers located in India and protected using modern security practices. The app is designed to follow global privacy standards such as GDPR and CCPA.
Q10: Where can I read the Privacy Policy and Terms?
A10: You can find direct links to the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions in the footer of every page on this website.
