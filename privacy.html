Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 02, 2025

FIM Viewer (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a product of Framed Interactive Media. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FIM Viewer mobile application and the .fim (Framed Interactive Media) interactive image format.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Analytics Data

FIM Viewer uploads certain analytics data to our secure servers located in India. This data helps users understand how their interactive images and hotspots are being used. Uploaded analytics may include:

  • Hotspot clicks
  • Hotspot link clicks
  • Total interaction counts per hotspot
  • Number of times a .fim file is opened
  • Other non-personal usage metrics

1.2 No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, payment details, or biometric data.

1.3 Subscription & Payment Data

All subscription payments and billing are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

1.4 Device Information

We may collect basic device-level data for app performance and security, including device model, OS version, and crash logs. Crash reports may be handled through Apple, Google, or platform SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use analytics data for:

  • Displaying hotspot interaction analytics within the app
  • Improving .fim format functionality
  • Understanding feature usage to improve the app

3. Data Storage & Security

Your analytics data is stored on secure servers located in India. We implement industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data.

4. Free Trial

FIM Viewer provides a 7-day free trial. During the trial, users have full access to Analytics features. After the trial, analytics access becomes part of a paid subscription.

5. Subscriptions

FIM Viewer offers auto-renewing subscriptions. Pricing varies by region and is displayed in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store at the time of purchase.

6. Advertising & Compliance

If in the future ads are shown on our website or content pages, we comply with Google AdSense, GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy rules. Some pages may use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party tags according to Google AdSense policies.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU Users)

You have the right to access, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your data.

Under CCPA (California Users)

California residents may request access, deletion, or opt-out of data sharing.

8. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained as long as your account remains active or as required for app functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

FIM Viewer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
